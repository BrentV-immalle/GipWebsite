TECHNO-QUIZ - THE COMPUTER VIRUS

Written by Brent Verlinden, Senne Weygers and Arne Meylemans

brent.verlinden@immalle.be
senne.weygers@immalle.be
arne.meylemans@immalle.be


FADE IN:

EXT. The school playground - Morning
You can see some trees and school building B.

Arne
Hello everyone!
We are three students who are very interested in IT-related stuff.
We decided to make a funny video about the dangers of computer viruses.
Of course, this video is ridiculous, but we want to make you aware of the risks.

Brent
You should never download anything suspicious on your computer.
This could result in someone stealing your data etc.
This video is about a quiz in which Mario and Luigi take part.
We all like to play video games, so those two characters seemed very appropriate for the video.
We hope you’ll enjoy the video!

Senne
In this video Brent Verlinden represents Mario.
Mario is a psychopath who wants to destroy the entire world.
Everyone thinks he’s a kind little boy, but he’s actually very dangerous and very good at coding.
He has the most stupid ideas. I represent Luigi. He knows Mario very well and has some stupid ideas too.
Arne Meylemans represents the presenter.

INT. Studio - Evening
The scene takes place in a small studio.
In this studio there are several computers and a small desk for the presenter.

While the intro sound is playing, you can see everyone walk towards their chair.

Presenter
Good evening everyone, welcome to the Techno-Quiz!
We're here today with Luigi and Mario.

Mario
It’s me, Mario!

Presenter
We'll start with the first question right away!
Soon you'll see a clip and you're supposed to guess how it ends.

Luigi
That's a piece of cake for me!

Mario
It’s me, Mario!

Presenter
This is the clip.

INT. Office - Evening
An office with a lot of computers in it. Three people are working in that office.

An employee asks another employee to explain something.

INT. Studio - Evening
The scene takes place in a small studio.
In this studio there are several computers and a small desk for the presenter.

Luigi
These computers are very old. I don't understand why these people still use them.
I know they're going to explode!

Presenter
Mario, what do you think?

Mario
I remember these computers from my childhood.
I remember that my first adventures started off with Donkey-Kong.

Presenter
Mario, what do you think is going to happen in the second part of the video?

Mario
I think the computer touched by the person on the right is going to fall.

Presenter
Is that your final answer? We’ll play the rest of the video and see if you were right.

INT. Office - Evening
An office with a lot of computers in it.
Three people are working in that office.

One of the employees stumbles over a cable.
As a result, a computer falls off a desk.

INT. Studio - Evening
The scene takes place in a small studio.
In this studio there are several computers and a small desk for the presenter.

Luigi
That person should be fired!

Presenter
That's too bad. Maybe you'll have better luck with the next question.
If you could make a computer virus all by yourself, what would it be capable of doing?

Luigi
Oh god, this one is easy.

Mario
My computer virus would be capable of destroying the entire world!

You hear a dream sound effect.
Creepy background sound.

DISSOLVE TO:

INT. Studio - Evening
The scene takes place in a small studio.
In this studio there are several computers and a small desk for the presenter.
The edges of the screen have faded. This way you can recognize that it's a dream.

Mario's going to sit behind a computer in the studio.

Luigi
Mario, what a good idea! Let’s do it!

Presenter
No, Mario, no! Don’t touch that computer!

Mario's programming a virus. He's typing on the keyboard.

Mario
Here we go!
We’re going to destroy the entire world! Mwahahaha!

Luigi
Don’t listen to him, Mario, let’s just do it! It’s the best idea ever!

Presenter
Mario, why would you do this?

Mario
Everyone thinks I’m just a dumb guy, but I’m actually a genius.
And now I’m going to prove everyone that I’m smarter than they think I am! Mwahahah!

Mario
Hey Luigi, which country should we destroy first?

Presenter
Stop it now! Do you even know what you’re doing? Get out of here, Luigi!

Luigi
Mario, can you proceed coding?

Mario
Uhm, Luigi? I think we have a big problem…

Thrilling background sound.

Presenter
Mario! What did you do! Everything is going to explode!

Luigi
Well, it all makes sense now.
These computers are as old as the ones in the clip you showed us!
I told you they were going to explode!

Mario
Don’t worry, everything is going to be alright.

Computer
Self-destruct in 30 seconds. (robotic voice effect)

Mario
Oh no, here we go again!

Presenter
We have to get out of here!

Luigi
You’re not going anywhere.

Mario
Well, are you happy now, sir?
The virus isn’t going to work, but we will all die very soon!

Computer
Self-destruct in 5 seconds. (robotic voice effect)

Presenter
Run for your life! Aaaaah!

They're rushing out of the studio.
The sound of an explosion.
Funny background sound.
